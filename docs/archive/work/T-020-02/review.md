### The substance of the prompt change

The old `## Honest-empty` section offered abstention as a **co-equal branch** ("If the fragment
closes NO vision-distance … ABSTAIN") — a low-friction off-ramp a cautious model took even on real
demand (the 33% false negative, T-019-02). The new section makes abstention a **rare, source-gated
exception**: a concrete test ("can you cite ONE real thing — a phrase, a file/doc/TODO, a run-log
fact? → extract; nothing to read → abstain"), a **calibrated example pair** (thin "water the office
plants" → ABSTAIN; grounded "run log records the bucket but not which gate" → EXTRACT), and an
explicit "roughness/terseness is never a reason to abstain." The `what`/`why` descriptions were
tightened to match at the type level (what the model sees via `{{ ctx.output_format }}`). This makes
honest-empty *collaborate* with the read-never-invent gate instead of competing with it — extract +
cite, or abstain on genuine emptiness.

## Open concerns (honest, IA-8)

1. **Budget-exhaustion appeared on 1/2 casts of *each* arm** — absent in T-019-02 (0/9). This is a
   **distinct** failure mode from honest-empty (the model churning/over-thinking on a long or
   off-topic context until it hits the 250k ceiling), **not** an AC regression — the AC is the
   gate-failed/honest-empty rate, which is met. Two readings: (a) small-N noise; (b) the sharper
   prompt makes the model *try harder* to ground rather than take the easy abstention off-ramp, which
   can push a borderline cast over budget. On the grounded arm this is arguably *correct engagement*
   (275k, barely over, and it was reading real demand). On the thin arm it is wasteful churn (381k on
   off-topic noise). **Recommend:** a follow-up sweep at higher N to see whether budget-exhaustion is
   noise or a real shift; if real on thin input, a future kaizen could nudge the model to abstain
   *fast* on obvious off-topic noise rather than churn. This is adjacent to sibling **T-020-03**
   (survey recalibration) and the stop-reason observability work the grounded fixture describes.
2. **N=2 is directional.** The over-fire elimination (33% → 0%) is clear at this N, but consistency
   across more casts is unmeasured. Reproduction commands are in `plan.md` / `progress.md`.
3. **No change to signal *content* dispersion** — T-019-02 also flagged expand's signal dispersion at
   0.50 (the "under-bind" half). That is **out of scope** for this ticket (which is honest-empty
   over-firing only) and remains an open E-020 concern for a separate pull.

## Verdict

The ticket's AC is **met**: grounded honest-empty rate driven to 0% (was 33%) while the thin
negative control still STOPs — tightened, not disabled. Deterministic gate green. The flagged
budget-exhaustion is a separate, lower-severity signal worth a higher-N follow-up, not a blocker.
