## Intent — the bigger-picture play

Vend's budget today speaks two denominations — tokens and wall-clock — and lane heat (src/play/lane-heat.ts) is deliberately RELATIVE: a lane reads hot only at ≥2× the coolest lane's burn, because the substrate cannot prove any lane is near an absolute cap; its own header defers reset-window quotas and cap/429 signals to exactly this follow-on. But the ratified two-seat operator's scarce unit is neither dollars nor raw tokens: each seat meters quota per reset window, and when a window is spent the lane 429s no matter what the token count says. This epic makes that unit a thing Vend KNOWS: the ledger captures the provider cap/429 event it currently has no field for, per-lane capacity per reset window is LEARNED from ledger history (observed burn between cap events and their cadence) rather than invented or hardcoded, and lane heat upgrades from relative-burn to quota-fraction — 'codex is at ~80% of its learned window' instead of 'codex burned 2× more than claude.' It matters NOW because E-071 shipped the overflow-aware default seat on the weaker relative signal and named this substrate as its follow-on demand: without absolute capacity, both-hot and both-cool lanes are indistinguishable, a near-cap lane keeps taking casts until it stalls them, and the practice ladder's last rung — scheduling across lanes — has no denominator to schedule against.

## Value to the design

Advances P7 by making the budget contract hard in the unit that actually binds the primary persona (quota-not-price economics): the ledger records when a lane's window was truly exhausted, and remaining capacity becomes a sourced, per-lane fact the clearing house allocates against — 'respects the budget both ways' now reads at the lane level, not just the run level. Advances P4 by giving seat inference decisive, absolute evidence: quota-fraction heat routes casts off a near-cap lane without an operator watching for 429s, closing the supervision gap the relative-burn guardrails (2× ratio, unrouted ties) had to leave open. For the core feature, this converts capacity scheduling from relative guesswork into allocation against known capacity — the prerequisite the whole overflow arc was waiting on.

## Done looks like

On a project whose ledger history contains cap/429 events, Vend can state each lane's learned capacity per reset window and its current quota-fraction, sourced entirely from the local ledger; a run that hits a provider cap lands in the ledger as a countable, honestly-named one-way marker (today it is invisible); and seat-inference provenance reads in quota-fraction terms ('claude at ~85% of learned window') instead of relative multiples. Where history contains no cap evidence, capacity is honestly unlearned: heat falls back to relative-burn, and the provenance says so — never an invented provider quota. Existing ledger records are untouched.

## Context & constraints

Ground truth in-repo: heat is src/play/lane-heat.ts (LANE_HEAT_WINDOW=100, HOT_LANE_RATIO=2), pure over RunRecord with a standing rule of never inventing provider quota facts — keep that rule, satisfy it with learned evidence. The ledger (src/log/run-log.ts) carries seatOfExecution plus the seatDefaulted/seatInferred one-way markers, whose established pattern the new cap/429 marker should follow; RUN_OUTCOMES has no rate-limit outcome today. The budget algebra is src/budget/budget.ts (Budget = tokens + timeMs) and src/budget/wallet.ts. PREREQUISITE named as prerequisite (PE-5): capacity can only be learned from cap/429 evidence, so capturing that marker at settle time is the first, in-scope substrate — and until real history accrues cap events, the learned denomination is dormant by design (the fallback keeps E-071's behavior byte-compatible). P5 respected: capacity is inferred from the local ledger only, never fetched from a provider API. N2: this is a denomination and an inference, not a live quota dashboard or monitor UI. N4: Vend learns and allocates; lisa executes — no runtime 429 interception or mid-run rerouting (E-071's deferred item (c) stays deferred). PE-7 right-sizing: this card stops at ledger-marker → learned capacity → quota-fraction heat, one ~2-hour block on established patterns; the wider signal's wallet-side surface — pricing counter-gesture envelopes IN quota (Budget/debitWave growing a lane-quota dimension across the spend algebra) — is a separate follow-on epic once this substrate exists, named here as E-071's item (d), not hidden scope. Out of scope: lanes beyond KNOWN_SEATS, provider-published quota lookups, rewriting historical records, and any change to how lisa dispatches.
