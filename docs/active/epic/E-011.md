## Intent — the bigger-picture play

Chain **ProposeEpic → DecomposeEpic** into one cast. Today the pipeline runs in two
by-hand steps — ProposeEpic mints an epic card, DecomposeEpic clears it to tickets (I
just did exactly this for E-010). This epic composes them: a demand signal in,
stories/tickets out, the epic card minted *and* cleared in between, **in one gesture**.
It is the engine's **first composition of plays** — a linear 2-node chain that threads
ProposeEpic's minted epic into DecomposeEpic's input. The multi-node DAG (E-007's
out-of-scope) generalizes from this minimal chain.

## Value to the design

Completes **"vend the whole roadmap"** — one gesture (**P2**) clears a raw demand signal
into harness-ready tickets, the clearing-house core feature end to end as a single cast.
Author-once (**P1**): the chain is defined once and cast forever. It is the capstone of
the signal → epic → tickets pipeline every prior play was building toward.

## Done looks like

- `vend` casts the chain (signal → ProposeEpic → epic card → DecomposeEpic →
  stories/tickets) in **one gesture**; both the epic card and its tickets are produced,
  gated, and logged.
- **Threading is real:** DecomposeEpic clears the *exact* epic ProposeEpic just minted.
- **End-to-end gating:** a gate STOP in *either* stage halts the chain — a ProposeEpic
  bounds STOP means DecomposeEpic never runs (no half-cleared output downstream).
- Both casts logged countably (two run-log records, one gesture).

## Context & constraints

- **Cites:** E-007 / `src/engine/cast.ts` (the engine + `castPlay` — the composition
  target); `src/play/propose-epic.ts` (`castProposeEpic`, E-009 — must now *surface* its
  minted epic path); `src/play/decompose-epic.ts` (DecomposeEpic takes an `epicPath`);
  `card-model.md` (composition); `src/play/note.ts` (the cast pattern).
- **Threading mechanism:** ProposeEpic's effect writes `E-0XX.md` but its `RunSummary`
  today carries only `{runId, outcome, materialized}`. The chain needs ProposeEpic to
  surface the **minted epic path** so it can feed it to DecomposeEpic's `epicPath`.
- **Non-goals:** the full multi-node DAG, branching, or parallel composition — this is a
  **linear 2-play chain** (the minimal composition; the DAG generalizes later). Not an
  executor (**N4**). PE-1 pull-discipline holds (one signal, not a board drain).
- **First slice:** the 2-play chain, cast in one gesture, output threaded, gated
  end-to-end. **Out:** arbitrary N-play DAGs, branching/parallel, the full
  `vend run <any-play>` generalization.
