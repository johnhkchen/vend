## Intent — the bigger-picture play

E-037 watched the macro-wallet live and **cleared 0** — `propose-epic` was guillotined at its
72,785 ms p90 timeout. E-038 **fixed that** (`timeoutMsFor × 2` headroom; a heavy signal now runs to

propose **finishes** where it died, decompose runs, a real epic+tickets mint, and the ledger gains

**The load-bearing lesson from E-037's sweep-log (shapes this run):** E-037's board #1 was a
**self-referential meta-signal** — *"Run and settle the E-037 live macro sweep…"*. propose-epic was
struggling to mint a clean epic for *the act of running the sweep itself* — a degenerate target, on
top of the tight timeout. **This run must test against a CONCRETE #1** — a real, decomposable product
signal — so a clear is *meaningful*, not a degenerate meta-clear. And unlike E-037 (when the board
was all "prove the wallet"), the board now carries **real product demand** (Frontier 7 `vend init`,
the frontiers), so a fresh steer/survey is more likely to surface a concrete top signal. If the
natural #1 is again self-referential, that is itself a **finding** (the loop pointed at an
introspective board mints degenerate targets) — record it, and clear the top **concrete** signal to
get a valid product-clear.
