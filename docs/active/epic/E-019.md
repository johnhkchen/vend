## Intent — the bigger-picture play

Vend's core promise is **consistency over a probabilistic process** — yet the
demand-extraction plays (expand E-016 / survey E-017 / steer E-018) have **unmeasured**
run-to-run variance, on the *widest* surface (steer reads the whole project). E-016 already
observed the warning sign: **3 casts of expand on one grounded fragment → 3 different
outcomes** (signal / budget-blow / over-eager honest-empty). This epic measures it:
generalize the decompose variance probe (`run-probe.ts`) to cast **any play N× on a fixed
input**, reporting **output variance** (the line-set-Jaccard metric, `variance.ts`) **and the
outcome mix** (signal / honest-empty / budget-exhausted rate). The honest-empty-over-eagerness
check is direct: on a **known-grounded** input, an honest-empty is a *false negative*.

## Value to the design

Quantifies the core value prop (**P3** — gates are the contract; here we measure the *output*
the contract is supposed to stabilize) on the plays the whole articulation lift now rests on.
A measurement, not a build — it returns a verdict (consistency acceptable, or the gates /
budgets need tuning), the E-014 discipline applied to the trilogy just shipped.

## Done looks like

- A **generalized consistency probe**: cast a named play **N× on a fixed input**, report (a)
  **run-to-run output variance** (reusing `variance.ts`'s Jaccard) and (b) the **outcome mix**
  (signal / honest-empty / budget-exhausted counts) — the honest-empty rate on a grounded
  input is the over-eagerness signal.
- The probe runs on **expand / survey / steer** (a bounded, cost-aware sweep — N small;
  survey/steer are heavy) into a **disposable temp ledger** (no real-log pollution, the
  `run-probe.ts` discipline).
- A **findings note**: per-play variance + honest-empty rate, and a verdict — is the
  articulation-play consistency acceptable, or do the gates (honest-empty/fork-genuineness) or
  budgets need tuning?

## Context & constraints

- **Cites:** `src/probe/run-probe.ts` (the harness to **generalize** — currently decompose-only,
  gated-vs-ungated; here it's any-play, run-to-run) + `src/probe/variance.ts` (the Jaccard
  metric to reuse); `src/play/expand-fragment.ts` / `survey.ts` / `steer.ts` (the plays under
  test, and their just-recalibrated budgets); E-014 (the measurement-initiative shape + the
  honest HOLD discipline); E-016 finding (2) (the 3-casts-3-outcomes warning).
- **Cost discipline:** survey/steer are heavy (300k/400k). Keep N small (e.g. 3) and the sweep
  bounded; log what was sampled (no silent caps). This is a *directional* read (small N), not
  a proof — say so (the E-014 honest-sample discipline).
- **Non-goals:** gated-vs-ungated for the articulation plays (the decompose probe did that;
  here the question is *run-to-run* on the same gates); a full statistical framework; auto-
  tuning the gates from the result (that's a follow-up, IA-14-style). Not an executor (**N4**).
- **First slice = the measurement** (~1 block): the generalized probe + the bounded sweep +
  the findings/verdict. **Out:** gate auto-tuning; the gated-vs-ungated arm.
