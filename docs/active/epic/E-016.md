## Intent — the bigger-picture play

The widest gap on the desk isn't *not knowing what to do* — it's the **extraction cost** of
turning a felt "this is rough" into an allocatable signal (`cognitive-overhead-articulation.md`
O1, 0.72). `expand-fragment` closes it: a rough fragment in, a **board-ready demand signal
out** — *what + why it might matter · value tier · a pre-filled budget envelope (measured,
E-013) · the invariant it advances*. It sits **one notch upstream of ProposeEpic** (the
pipeline grows `fragment → expand → signal → propose → epic → decompose → tickets`) and is
**the primitive the rest of the articulation lift reuses** (Survey, latent-harvest, "what's
next"). It is `clearing-dynamics.md` made real: the human holds **intent and assent**; Vend
holds the **articulation**.

## Value to the design

Collapses the human's entry cost to *edit a draft, not compose from blank* (**P2** — fewer
gestures; the cognitive-overhead headline). And it's **not trust-gated** — it ships while the
macro-wallet stays parked on E-014's E1 HOLD, *and* by getting the builder running Vend with
less friction it **accrues the E1 walk-away data that ungates the wallet** (the virtuous
loop). Another play on the proven engine — the v1 surface widening, not new substrate.

## Done looks like

- A `vend expand "<fragment>"` gesture casts the play: a rough fragment + project context →
  **a structured signal** (what · why · tier · pre-filled measured budget · `advances`),
  rendered in the `demand.md` signal shape, **staged** (not auto-promoted) for a human pull.
- **Read, never invent (the signature gate, PE-1):** the signal must trace to real project
  state / the fragment; speculation is refused. A fragment that closes **no** vision-distance
  yields an **honest empty** result (IA-4), not manufactured busywork.
- Cast through the one metered seam — gated, budgeted, run-logged like every play.
