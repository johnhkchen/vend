## Intent — the bigger-picture play

Productize **this session's steering process** — `project-steering.md`'s 10 moves — into a
single play. Where `Survey` (E-017) reads the project and proposes a **ranked board** (the
*what*), `SteerProject-lite` adds the **forks** (the *decisions*): the handful of genuine
choices only a human can make — the trade-offs, the sequencing calls, the values forks (e.g.
*"build the wallet now or measure trust first?"*, *"deeper-per-epic or chain-more-epics?"*) —
each with its options and why it matters. One gesture in, a **steer** out: the board **and**
the real forks. It is `clearing-dynamics.md` realized — the human's role collapses to
**author + assent**; Vend holds the extraction, the ranking, the pricing, and even the
*framing of the decisions*. It is the third and last brick of automating the **STEER** stage.

## Value to the design

Completes the automated **steer** (the cycle's one remaining by-hand end): the survey-and-
surface-the-forks move I have run by hand every loop this session, made a play (**P2** — the
human assents to a handful of forks instead of composing from scratch). Not trust-gated;
ships while the macro-wallet is parked. The capstone the whole articulation lift was built
toward — *the fruit-picker itself.*

## Done looks like

- A `vend steer` gesture casts the play: project state → a **steer** staged for the human —
  a **ranked board** (reusing E-016's `Signal`) **and** a small set of **`Fork`s** (each: the
  question · 2–4 options · why it matters · a recommendation).
- **Surface real forks, never manufacture a decision (the signature gate):** a `Fork` must be
  a *genuine* trade-off the human must make; a fake/inconsequential choice is refused. If the
  path is clear, **surface no forks** — don't invent a decision to seem useful (the fork-side
  sibling of honest-empty / read-never-invent).
- Board signals grounded (read-never-invent, PE-1); cast through the one metered seam — gated,
  budgeted, run-logged.
