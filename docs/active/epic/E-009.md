## Intent — the bigger-picture play

Register **ProposeEpic** (the play above `DecomposeEpic`) as a real play on the E-007
casting engine. Author its BAML function (demand signal → epic card), its PE gates
(value / bounds / structural, the structural one reusing **E-004's id-guard** to keep
the minted epic id disjoint from the board), its effect (write the epic card), and
register it. Once registered, casting ProposeEpic on a demand signal produces a valid
`E-0XX.md` card — which `DecomposeEpic` then clears into tickets. The full
**signal → epic → tickets** pipeline, every step a typed cast through one engine.

## Value to the design

Completes the core feature end to end and advances **author-once-run-forever** (**P1**)
— ProposeEpic is specified once (`propose-epic.md`, PE-1–PE-7) and cast forever. It is
also the first *valuable* non-DecomposeEpic play on the engine (CaptureNote proved the
engine; ProposeEpic uses it for real), exercising the engine's generality with a real,
gated, card-writing play.

## Done looks like

- `baml_src/propose.baml`: `ProposeEpic(signal, charter, project) -> EpicCard`, authored
  + generated.
- `proposeEpicPlay` registered on the engine; casts through the **same** `castPlay` as

- a live cast on a real demand signal writes a valid `E-0XX.md` whose **id is disjoint**
  from the board (structural gate via E-004's guard), gated by PE value/bounds, logged
  countably.
- the pipeline shown: a signal → ProposeEpic → an epic card (which DecomposeEpic could
  then clear to tickets).

## Context & constraints

- **Cites:** `playbooks/propose-epic.md` (PE-1–PE-7, the gates, the pull-discipline

  `src/baml/decompose-bridge.ts` + `baml_src/decompose.baml` (the BAML render/parse
  pattern); `src/play/id-guard.ts` (`detectCollisions` — reuse for epic-id disjointness).
- **Prerequisites (met):** the engine (E-007), the ProposeEpic spec, the id-guard (E-004).
- **Pull-discipline (PE-1, load-bearing):** ProposeEpic fires on a *pulled* signal,
  invoked deliberately — **never** an auto-drainer over the whole board (overproduction).
- **Non-goals:** not the auto-drainer (PE-1); not the full `vend run <any-play>`
  generalization — ProposeEpic registers with its own `castProposeEpic` entry, like
  CaptureNote (that generalization is a separate follow-up).
- **First slice:** register ProposeEpic + cast it live on one signal. **Out:** chaining
  ProposeEpic → DecomposeEpic automatically (signal → epic → tickets in one press), and
  the input-assembly generalization.
