  the E-039 budget that cleared 2). **NOT** a free proof.
- **Crossing 10 records meets the PRE-WIRED bar — state it, but don't inflate it.** The E-014/E-026
  caveat stands: the trust evidence is **"didn't-break," not "stress-tested"** (most forward records
  are walk-aways that didn't *need* intervention; only 1 genuine `--intervened` so far). So the settle
  may **upgrade the go from provisional toward met** per the pre-wired rule, while **stating the
  composition honestly** (cleared vs censored vs genuinely-tested). **No "bulletproof" over-claim**

- **It may clear fewer than hoped — honest if so.** A 1-clear (→10) still crosses; a 0-clear (regression)
