## Intent — the bigger-picture play

E-014's verdict already moved **HOLD → go** this session (the measurement sprint, `work/measurement-
sprint/findings.md`) — but on **weak evidence**: the E1 walk-away number (100%, 13/13) was a
*post-hoc, uniform, single-attestor back-fill* with **zero `--intervened` records**, so it shows
"nothing forced a stop," not "trust calibrated against tempting-to-intervene cases." The go was taken
*with that caveat stated*, and the wallet (E-024/E-025) shipped with its hard-stops intact precisely
because the evidence was *didn't-break*, not *stress-tested*. This epic collects the **real forward
E1** the back-fill structurally couldn't be: ≥10 genuine `vend run --intervened/--no-intervened`
sessions (with actual variance), then `vend audit` for the walk-away rate + trend, then **confirm the
provisional go on strong evidence — or surface the reroute the weak sample masked.** Measurement, not
feature work: read the number, render the verdict, build nothing beyond the read. (E2 consistency is
already read at ~21%; this is the E1 half, *hardened* rather than first-collected.) **The macro-wallet
is a forward E1 source itself** — every `vend work` cast left to clear is a real walk-away to record.

## Value to the design

Produces the single most important number in the product's case — does the author actually walk away? — and converts it into a binding go/reroute verdict on the headline two-gesture autonomy mechanic. Advances P4 (autonomy is the bet; this proves or disproves it before more autonomy is built on top) and P3 (the gates' trust contract is quantified from real run data, not asserted). It is the evidence gate that lets the keystone ship or reroute on fact rather than hope.

## Done looks like

≥10 real `vend run` sessions (a mix of --intervened/--no-intervened, **including the macro-wallet's
own autonomous casts**) are recorded in the run ledger; `vend audit` reports a walk-away rate plus its
trend over those sessions against the IA-12 andon budget; and the **provisional go is reaffirmed on
this strong, variance-bearing evidence — or revised to a reroute** in a one-page recorded verdict
citing the measured rate. Observable: the audit number exists, traces to ≥10 genuine sessions (not the
back-fill), and the E-014 go-verdict now stands on forward data rather than a post-hoc attestation.

## Context & constraints

Grounds against: E-014 (done — instruments shipped, verdict HOLD pending E1 data; advances P3,P4); E-024/E-025 (macro-wallet built — `vend work` casts at wallet-reserved price, full check gate green 830/0). Prerequisites, named as prerequisites not hidden scope: (a) the E-014 instruments — `vend run --intervened/--no-intervened` and `vend audit` — must be functional; if a real-session run path is missing or flaky, that is a blocking prerequisite to surface, not in-scope build. The sessions must be REAL casts, not synthetic ledger entries — a thin or fabricated sample fails the gate's whole purpose. Out of scope: any go/reroute REMEDIATION (rebuilding or reparking the wallet) is a downstream epic chosen by this sprint's verdict, not part of it; no new instruments or dashboards (N2 — not a babysitting dashboard; measure, don't surveil). Right-sizing (PE-7): orchestration and audit are ~one 2-hour block, but casting ≥10 real sessions is wall-clock-heavy and partly bounded by run latency — if real-session throughput proves slow, the casting may need to run as a bounded background sweep across more than one sitting; flag rather than inflate the card.
