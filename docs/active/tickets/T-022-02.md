## Context

Run the equivalence judge (T-022-01) on expand/survey/steer as a **bounded sweep** (N≈3,
cost-aware), then synthesize the **consistency contract** — the decision the epic exists for.
Capture, as a durable note in `information-architecture.md` (a new IA principle), **what
consistency Vend promises**: *validity / grounding consistency via the gates, not lexical
identity* — and, **per play**, the verdict:

- **by-design** where the dispersion is *equivalent-diversity* (valid outputs that mean the
  same thing — divergence is acceptable, and we say so);
- **converge** where it is *genuine-disagreement* (the play proposes different things run-to-
  run — name the concrete downstream convergence epic to pull).

Surface the **human fork** for assent (converge-vs-by-design, per play), recommendation-first
— the deciding call is the human's, the evidence and the recommendation are ours.

**Cites:** T-022-01 (the diagnosis); `vision.md` ("consistency over a probabilistic process");
`information-architecture.md` (where the contract principle lands); E-014 (the validity axis,
≈21%); `demand.md` (the bridge — a "converge" verdict becomes a downstream signal).
