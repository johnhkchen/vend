## Context

Wire the pure model (T-003-01) to reality: gather the structured inputs (parse
`demand.md` signals + `lisa status` readiness), then rank → render → **persist to
`.vend/menu.json`** and print. Bare `vend` (no args) becomes the browse surface.
Persisting is what makes the indices stable between `vend` (show) and `vend <sel>`
(pick). Follow the impure-verb / pure-shaping split (rule R1).

**Cites:** `epic/E-003.md` (the `.vend/menu.json` index-stability seam);
`src/cli.ts` (the entry from `T-002-03` to extend); `src/play/project-context.ts`
(the existing-state reading pattern — `listIds`, `lisa` snapshot).
