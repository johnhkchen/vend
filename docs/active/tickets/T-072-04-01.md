## Context

Three successful decompose runs logged 16, 17, and 18 turns against the pinned 15-turn cap (`turns: 18 / 15 cap`) — the cap is either not binding or the displayed counter measures a different unit than the cap bounds. Diagnose which at the cast↔executor seam, then make the surface honest: a successful run must never display a turn count that exceeds its own cap in like terms.

_Advances: P3 — Gates are the contract (a displayed cap that doesn't bind is a contract that lies); P7 — Budget is a hard contract (turns are the third budget axis alongside time and tokens)_
