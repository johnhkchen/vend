## Context

The decompose BAML function's story output carries only a title and ticket ids today, so the
materializer has nothing to write but a shell. Extend the story type in
`src/baml/decompose-bridge.ts` (and the `.baml` schema it bridges) with the five contract fields —
`scope`, `storyAcceptance`, `honestBoundary`, `waveRationale`, `outOfSlice` — and extend the play's
render in `src/play/decompose-epic-core.ts` to demand them, showing `docs/active/stories/S-066-01.md`
itself as the in-prompt exemplar of the expected shape. Typed end to end: a story missing a field
surfaces as a typed absence for the downstream gate to refuse — never a silently fabricated default.

_Advances: P3 (gates are the contract — the schema is what the gate can hold the output to) and
P1 (author once — the contract demand is paid here, in the playbook, and amortizes over every cast)._

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] `bun test` fixtures round-trip a story through parse with all five contract fields populated;
      a fixture omitting any field parses to a typed absence, not a default.
- [ ] A render snapshot test asserts the prompt demands all five sections and embeds the exemplar
      shape.
