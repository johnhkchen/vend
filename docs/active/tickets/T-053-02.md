## Context

**Confirm the band makes the budget rational end-to-end — through the real cast-funding path, not just the
pure core.** T-053-01 added the `[350k, 700k]` clamp inside `fundingEnvelope`; because E-050/T-050-02
already threaded `fundingEnvelope` into `work.ts` (the macro loop) and the chain's
`resolveStepBudgets`/`fundedStepDefault`, the band flows through both paths automatically. This ticket
proves the two rational-band outcomes the epic exists for, and that nothing else moved.

**What to confirm (deterministic, stub records — NO live model):**
1. **The floor fixes the `vend chain` halt.** A well-calibrated `propose-epic` (p90 ~170k — the exact
   shape that budget-exhausted at 176,101/169,873) is now FUNDED at the 350k floor through the cast funding
   path (`resolveStepBudgets`/`fundedStepDefault`), so a 176k actual no longer exhausts — the tail-draw halt
   cannot recur. Assert the funded step budget is ≥ 350k for that ledger fixture.
2. **The ceiling caps the runaway.** An under-calibrated `decompose-epic` whose `fundingEnvelope` would
   compute ~733k (E-051 shape: a censored record × headroom) is FUNDED at exactly the 700k ceiling, not 733k.
3. **GUARD ≠ PRICE held (IA-8 + P7).** The macro-wallet still AUTHORIZES on the honest `price` (the p90 sum,
   NOT the banded funding) and DEBITS actuals — confirm `canAfford`/`fitNext` see the unbanded price and the
   shelf/`formatEnvelopeLabel` quote is unchanged; the band changed only what a cast RUNS under.

**No production behavior beyond the band** — this ticket is mostly the assertions that pin the rational
outcomes (and guard against a regression that re-banded the price or the authorization). If a test-only
helper is needed to drive `resolveStepBudgets` with a stub ledger, that is in scope; no `src` change beyond
what T-053-01 made (the band auto-flows).
