guillotined at p90 → `timed-out` → censored OUT of the sample that sets the envelope. Raising the
percentile can't fix it (the tail is censored from its own sample); the fix is **headroom on the

warranted, documented constant) so the per-cast runaway-guard has slack above the price — while
price, affordability (`fitNext`), the shelf, and accounting keep reading the honest measured p90
(IA-8). Deterministic proof maps to E-037's ~72–73 s censored data (those runs would finish under the
headroomed timeout); the ratchet + IA-14-deferred rationale are documented. Single-ticket epic (the
E-027 precedent). Epic-scoped ids (R6 — `038` clean).
