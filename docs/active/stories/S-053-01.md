Part of **epic E-053 (rational-funding-band)** — clamp the per-cast funding guard (`fundingEnvelope`,
E-050) to a rational BAND: a **FLOOR of 350,000 tokens** (no cast funded below it, so a too-tight p90
never starves a real cast — the failure `vend chain`'s propose just hit: budget-exhausted at 176,101
against a 169,873 envelope, over by 3.6%) and a **CEILING of 700,000 tokens** (the hard P7 wall — runaway
self-funding, E-051's decompose ran to ~733k, is rejected here). "Care at 350k, reject at 700k."

The band is on the FUNDING guard only — the token output of `fundingEnvelope`. The quoted price / shelf /
`formatEnvelopeLabel` stays the honest p90 (IA-8); the macro-wallet keeps authorizing on price and
debiting actuals (work.ts:194-199 — GUARD ≠ PRICE). Since `fundingEnvelope` is already threaded into
`work.ts` and the chain's `resolveStepBudgets`/`fundedStepDefault` (E-050/T-050-02), the band flows
through both paths automatically — no new wiring, just the clamp. The completion of the E-038 (time
headroom) → E-050 (cold-start widening) calibration arc: generous, but within sane bounds.

Pure band → confirm it: T-053-01 (`FUNDING_FLOOR_TOKENS`/`FUNDING_CEILING_TOKENS` + clamp the token
output to `[350k, 700k]` after the existing max(); below-floor → floored, above-ceiling → capped, in-band
→ unchanged, price + wall-clock untouched; PURE, unit-tested) → T-053-02 (confirm end-to-end: propose's
~170k p90 funds at the 350k floor through the cast funding path so the tail-exhaustion can't recur;
decompose capped at 700k; macro-wallet authorizes on price + debits actuals unchanged; deterministic
proof). FREE/deterministic, no live model.

```
T-053-01  pure band — FUNDING_FLOOR_TOKENS (350k) + FUNDING_CEILING_TOKENS (700k); clamp fundingEnvelope's token output to [floor, ceiling]; price + wall-clock untouched; unit-tested
   └─ T-053-02  confirm end-to-end — propose funds at the 350k floor (tail-exhaustion can't recur), decompose capped at 700k, macro-wallet authorizes on price + debits actuals (IA-8 + P7); deterministic
```

**Out of this slice:** any band on the PRICE (IA-8 — the shelf stays the honest p90); changes to
`canAfford`/`fitNext` authorization (gates on price, unchanged); a wall-clock band (the policy's numbers
are tokens; time keeps its E-038 headroom); making the floor/ceiling data-driven (they are pinned
constants — tunable later, not now); the "a play's honest p90 exceeds the ceiling" case (none does today).
