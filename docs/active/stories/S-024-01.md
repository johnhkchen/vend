Part of **epic E-024 (macro-wallet)** — the founding gesture made literal: fund a feature-block
budget once, and Vend spends it down autonomously across casts (pull/chain the ranked board, fit
each next cast into the remaining wallet at its **measured** price, stop on exhausted/cleared/andon).
The rung above E-013 (measured envelopes are the prerequisite); un-gated by E-014's **go**. Walk-away
autonomy (P4) on a depleting hard budget (P7).

Build bottom-up: the depleting wallet (T-024-01, pure, P7), then the autonomous spend loop
(T-024-02, the fit-next/should-continue decision over the impure loop, P4), then the counter gesture
(T-024-03, `vend work` — Confirm→Run→Settle at macro scale). Epic-scoped ids (R6 — `024` clean).

## DAG

```
T-024-01  the depleting wallet (pure: allocate / canAfford / debit / remaining; two denominations)
   └─ T-024-02  the autonomous spend loop (fit-next + should-continue; drives castChain, debits actuals)
        └─ T-024-03  the counter gesture — `vend work --budget <ms>,<tokens>` (run loop + Settle)
```

**Out of this slice:** a smarter pull optimizer / lookahead (leverage rank is the policy); parallel
/ fleet spending (one line at a time); a GUI budget dial (the CLI is v1); re-deriving per-cast prices
(E-013 owns them).
