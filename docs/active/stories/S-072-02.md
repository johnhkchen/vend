**Scope:** The live-surface seam — src/engine/cast-core.ts (a new pure progress accumulator: pull per-turn `usage` off a StreamMessage, weight it via budget.ts `countTokens`/`COST_WEIGHTS`, and format elapsed · weighted-spend/envelope · turn humanely, reusing the menu.ts humane-unit idiom) and src/engine/cast.ts (drive it from the existing makeStreamSink onMessage path, threading the funded envelope and a clock into one updating stdout line). Reads existing streamed events; adds none.

**Story acceptance:** During a metered cast the live surface shows ONE updating line — elapsed time, price-true weighted spend against the funded envelope, and turn count — in place of the ~90 bare `· system (thinking_tokens)` event names; fed a fixture StreamMessage sequence, the pure accumulator yields the expected humane line at each step (cast-core.test.ts); the durable per-run transcript (.vend/transcripts/<runId>.jsonl) still receives every raw message unchanged.

**Honest boundary:** The accumulator + formatter is FREE and fixture-proven (no tokens); the single-line live refresh is proven by the wiring ticket and confirmed on the next real metered cast, authorized at the counter — named here, not hidden in a ticket. Live mid-cast spend is READ from streamed per-turn usage and is an approximation that converges to the result message's authoritative final `usage`; the raw transcript stays the source of truth. Detached/notify and the silent-death-under-shell-timeout fix are explicitly deferred (their own design pull, per the epic).
