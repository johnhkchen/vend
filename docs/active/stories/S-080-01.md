**Scope:** The lisa→vend seam recorder and its consumption edge: src/seam/lisa-loop-settled-core.ts (marker schema + event classification), src/seam/lisa-loop-settled.ts (recorder effect + new trace append), src/seam/fixtures/lisa-loop-settled.valid.json, and the loop/cord lines of settle's surface (src/settle/settle-core.ts verdict shape, src/settle/settle.ts render). The .lisa hook scripts and lisa itself are untouched.

**Story acceptance:** Running the recorder with LISA_DURATION_SECS unset but every other fact valid publishes a claimable loop-settled marker, and the next settle prints its loop line without a fabricated duration; running it with a refusable input (relative LISA_PROJECT, non-numeric ticket count) appends one timestamp+reason line to the gitignored seam log under .vend/ and the next `vend settle` prints 'cord: last recording failed — <reason>'; a settle whose successful claim is newer than the log prints no cord warning. All proven by tests through fixture inputs.

**Honest boundary:** Everything here is fixture/unit-proven and FREE — no tokens, no live lisa loop; re-running the 2026-07-13 end-to-end trial bare is the epic's closing evidence, deferred to epic closeout and named here. Also honest: the trace narrows the silent window, it cannot eliminate it — a recorder process that dies before it boots, or a hook that never spawns it, still leaves no line; the hook's swallow-errors never-block-lisa containment is deliberately unchanged.
