**Scope:** Mid-cast draft persistence and resume — a new versioned draft store under `.vend/` written in castPlay after parse+gates (src/engine/cast.ts ~348), its clear-on-success lifecycle (mirroring cast-diff capture→reconcile, cast.ts:400/581), a doctor probeResumableDecompose (mirroring src/doctor/board-hygiene-probe.ts, wired at cli.ts:1050-1054 alongside probeBoardHygiene), and a `--resume` path (parseRunArgs → assembleAndCast → castPlay bypass-dispense).

**Story acceptance:** An interrupted/failed decompose leaves a readable draft (epic + parsed draft + gate findings + next-repair-action); `vend doctor` reports a red `resumable-decompose: <epic>` Check with the resume command in its hint; `vend run decompose-epic <epic> --resume` re-enters at gates/effect from the draft with no cold executor dispense; a clean run leaves no draft.

**Honest boundary:** The persisted state and resume are exercised through the stub executor / fixture casts and are FREE; the live metered long-tail interruption of the reporter's real cast is not re-run here. Resumable ≠ auto-repair: this story persists and re-enters a draft; it builds no regeneration loop (none exists, per the epic). It persists gate findings AS-IS and does not redefine the degrade taxonomy (that is S-077-02).
