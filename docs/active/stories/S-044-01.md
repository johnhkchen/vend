Part of **epic E-044 (steer-ranker-concrete-demand)** — recalibrate the steer/survey ranker so the
autonomous loop clears concrete product work, not meta-tasks. Both live sweeps (E-037, E-039) had
`vend steer` rank a self-referential "run the sweep" signal #1; the operator re-pointed by hand, but
an unattended operator (P4) can't. Steer ranks via the BAML model; the pure gates are structural
(they "cannot judge semantic consequence"), so the self-referential signal passes them all. The fix
is a **prompt recalibration** (the E-020 honest-empty precedent), not a brittle keyword gate.

One surgical ticket: T-044-01 recalibrates `steer.baml` + `survey.baml` so a demand signal must be
concrete product demand (decomposable into an epic) and self-referential/operational meta-tasks are
demoted beneath it or excluded. Deterministic proof: `baml:gen` green + a contract assertion the
steering is in place; structural gates unchanged. Live confirmation (the model now ranks a concrete #1)
is deferred to the next steer cast — the E-038→E-039 shape. Single-ticket epic. Epic-scoped ids (R6 —
`044` clean).
