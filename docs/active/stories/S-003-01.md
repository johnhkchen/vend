Part of **epic E-003 (vend-cli-shelf)** — the shelf you *browse*: bare `vend`
renders a numbered menu of salient, ready, high-leverage actions, computed
**deterministically** (no LLM) from `demand.md` + `lisa status`, persisted to
`.vend/menu.json` for index stability.

Epic-scoped ids per the rule-set (`playbook-decompose-epic.md` R6). Pure model
before integration (R1).

## DAG

```
T-003-01  pure-menu-model (rank + render)   (no deps)
   └─ T-003-02  gather-state · persist · bare `vend` entry
```

**Out of scope** (E-003): LLM-precomputed salience (`.vend/menu.json` is the seam
for it later), concurrency, filters beyond `--all`, the full TUI.
