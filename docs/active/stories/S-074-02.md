**Scope:** The funding counter where a budget meets a play before dispatch — src/shelf/press-core.ts / press.ts and the shared dispatch node (dispatch.ts `runPlay`) that both `vend <sel>` and `vend steer` fund a cast through — plus a new pure underfunding-decision core. It recalibrates the play's measured envelope floor via the existing shelfRows/recalibrate machinery (src/shelf/shelf-row.ts, src/ledger/recalibrate.ts) and compares the funded budget against it. Slice 3. The executor boundary and cast andon are untouched.

**Story acceptance:** A cast funded far below the play's shelf-computed measured envelope floor (the field-report case: steer funded at 12.5k against a ~400k measured floor) prints an underfunding warning naming the funded amount vs the floor BEFORE dispensing, then PROCEEDS if the operator continues (warn, not block, per E-068). A cast funded at/near its floor prints nothing and is byte-identical to today.

**Honest boundary:** The warning fires only when the play HAS a measured floor (recalibrate returns `source: measured`); a cold-start play with no run history has no floor to warn against and is deliberately NOT warned — named here, not hidden. The warning is FREE (no tokens) and computed from the already-read run log. Live proof against the real ~400k steer envelope is the deferred field-report verify.
