## The strategic read

The **macro-wallet** — the headline autonomy gesture — is **parked** (E-014's E1 walk-away
unmeasured → HOLD). That's not a stall: the cycle found the **widest gap on the desk is
articulation, not autonomy** (O1 *"I can feel the work but can't formulate it"*, opportunity

ungates the wallet**. The articulation lift is doubly justified: it's the available work
